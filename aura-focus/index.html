What's Inside

Designed to disappear

Every interaction is intentional. The interface fades so your focus can deepen.

🌧️

Lo-Fi Ambient Environments

Seamless looping background videos paired with calibrated ambient soundscapes. Rainy nights, windy fields, cozy windows — each theme creates its own sensory space.

👆

Gesture-Driven Timer

Double-tap to compact. Long-press to drag. No buttons to hunt for. Your timer persists even if the app restarts — you never lose a session.

🌙

Auto-Dim Focus UI

The interface fades when you stop interacting. Configure the delay and intensity to match your personal workflow rhythm.

📊

Focus Analytics

Understand your patterns. Weekly focus minutes, completion rates, and which themes help you reach the deepest flow state.

🌌

Live Constellation

See how many people around the world are currently in deep focus. You are never focusing alone — it is a shared ritual.

🔔

Abandonment Recovery

Walk away mid-session? Gentle (or cheeky) notifications remind you. Return in time and pick up right where you left off.
